forage4food

forage4food is a web application developed by Victor Mao using Yelp's Fusion API, Heroku, Django, and Python for Capital One's Software Engineering Summit. The goal of forage4food is to help users pick a restaurant.

How 2 Use:

Enter a restaurant or food category and click "Forage!". You will be shown an overview of the top 4 nearby food destinations relating to your search. Click on the address for Google Map Directions. Click on reviews for Yelp Reviews.
